Frequently asked questions

Simple answers before you submit a request.

When should we begin preparing for renewal?

Many organizations begin 90 to 120 days before renewal, especially if an RFP or board review is involved.

What documents should we have ready?

Helpful items include current policies, schedules, loss runs, payroll estimates, vehicle lists, property values, and contracts.

Can you review our current insurance before we issue an RFP?

Yes. An early review can help clarify missing information and questions before bids are compared.

Which types of public organizations do you work with?

We focus on schools, municipalities, authorities, districts, counties, and organizations serving public contracts.
